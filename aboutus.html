ABOUT JnC

JnC Bikeshop was established in 2023, located in Cavite, Philippines. The company is a professional bicycle company integrating product research and development, sales and service.

Company is branded JnC. Which means the J and C.The J is represents our unwavering commitment to Journey, Innovation, and Customer Joy. C symbolizes our unwavering commitment to Customer Satisfaction, Creativity, and Community. J and C is also the first names of the founder of the brand JD and Christian, Committed to the development of the JnC brand.

Products Offerings:

BICYCLES

At JnC Bikeshope, we offer a diverse selection of bikes from renowned brands. Our inventory includes mountain bikes, road bikes, hybrid bikes, electric bikes, and more.

BIKE PARTS

We understand the importance of maintaining and upgrading bikes. Therefore, we stock a comprehensive range of high-quality bike parts. Customers can find components such as frames, wheels, tires, pedals, gears, brakes, chains, and more.

BIKE ACCESSORIES

To enhance the biking experience and meet various needs, JnC Bikeshope offers an extensive range of accessories. We have a wide selection of helmets, lights, locks, bike racks, water bottle cages, cycling apparel, gloves, and other accessories that ensure safety, comfort, and convenience while riding.
